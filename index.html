What Even is This?

Hey! Its okay to be confused on what this thing is. I wont go in much depth about the theory, but this is called "Game of Life" which was invented by Jhon Horton Conway and it is a Cellular Automata
You can read more more about the theory from the Wikipedia Article.

How Do I Play?

Well, you dont (in a sense). Game of life is designed to be played automatically from a starting state.
Example: The start state here are two Gasper Guns that generate / fire Gliders for indefinite amount of time.

What do I Do?

  • Click on a Position on Grid.
  • If the cell is alive, it will be colored in any color you choose from color picker
  • Click on Play Game
  • If you want to stop the simulation, click on Stop Game
  • To Clear the Board, click on Clear Board (After Stopping the Game)
